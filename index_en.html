Wedding

Civil Wedding
(May 4th, 2017 - 4:30 pm)
With the small circle of our family, the bridesmaid and the best men, we’re celebrating the civil wedding on May 4th. The ceremony takes place in our second home town Bottmingen (CH) in the local castle.
Chapel
(June 24th, 2017 - 12:30 pm)
The wedding takes place in a chapel in Lauf an der Pegnitz (Germany). Everybody is cordially invited to come. After the ceremony there is a small aperitif and our photographer is going to take a group picture in front of the chapel.
Dinner & Party
(June 24th, 2017 - 3:15 pm)
From the chapel we’re heading directly to the dinner & party at the castle barn in Stolzenroth. There is plenty of Franconian food and local brew available. The celebration is open-end and is accompanied by our band. So be prepared to party (a lot).
Honeymoon
(July 8th - August 11th, 2017)
For our five week honeymoon we’re saying "no worries” and are heading down under. Unfortunately, you can’t come. However, if you’re still wondering about a wedding gift :-)... In return we (might) show you some pictures.
